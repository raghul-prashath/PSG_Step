Application

Apply Now

Application

  • The call for application opens on 1 Sep 2022 and the last date for submission of applications is 30 Sep 2022.

  • Participants are requested to fill an online application form where they have to give details of their company, problem they are trying to address, Business model etc.

  • Program starts on 24 Oct 2022

Selection

  • After a preliminary administrative check, the applications that clear the administrative check will be evaluated for criteria such as technical strength, commercialisation potential, value proposition and go-to-market strategy, scalability and sustainability.

  • The Selection Committee will select the Cohort for the Accelerator Program.

  • 15 start-ups will be selected for this Accelerator Program.

Criteria for Selection

The Selection Committee will shortlist applications based on:

  • Technical strength

  • Commercialisation potential

  • Value proposition

  • Go-to-market strategy

  • Scalability

  • Sustainability

Demo Day

At the end of the 12 week Accelerator Program, a Demo Day will be conducted where the start-ups will make presentations to an invited audience of Investors, potential customers and other partners.
